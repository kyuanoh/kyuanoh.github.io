Research Vision

My research vision is to develop intelligent agents that can understand the physical world, predict how it evolves, and act reliably through grounded multimodal reasoning.

I am particularly interested in vision-language-action models, physical grounding, omnimodal intelligence, and world models. Ultimately, I aim to build systems that unify perception, reasoning, prediction, and action across diverse sensory and embodied environments.
